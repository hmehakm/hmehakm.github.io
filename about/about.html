About Me

UX Researcher Shaping
Agile Product Decisions.

View My Work Get In Touch

Background

I work across UX research, product strategy, and business decision making, with more than 8 years of experience in business and product environments. This background helps me understand how teams actually make decisions and how research can influence what gets prioritized and built.

Approach

I design research to fit real product delivery environments, especially Agile teams. I focus on clear scope definition, strong stakeholder alignment, and methods that match decision needs. My goal is to ensure research outputs directly support product tradeoffs rather than sit as standalone insights.

Moving Forward

I aim to build research practices that are deeply embedded in product development, where user understanding continuously informs strategy, prioritization, and design decisions in real time.
