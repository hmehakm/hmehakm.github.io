Hello, I’m Mehak — User Experience Researcher

I believe consistency and hard work are essential to exploring and developing skills. You’ll see my strengths — and you’ll also see me improving my weaknesses. I’m eager to take on new projects to showcase what I know and discover what I don’t.

I’m a Chicago-based researcher focused on inclusive, data-driven design. I translate lived experiences into actionable insights and simple, testable solutions.

My practice blends qualitative research, accessibility heuristics, and rapid prototyping to create products that respect users’ time and dignity.

“Small interventions — a vibration, a clear status, an alt text — can restore independence and confidence at scale.”
